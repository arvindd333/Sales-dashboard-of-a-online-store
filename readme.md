# Sales Dashboard of an Online Store

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive sales dashboard created for data analysis practice and business intelligence reporting. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, revenue trends, customer orders, and product performance for an online store.

The dashboard was designed to help understand:
- Revenue distribution across countries
- Monthly sales and order trends
- Product-wise sales performance
- Yearly revenue and order comparison
- Customer purchasing patterns

This project demonstrates skills in:
- Data Cleaning
- Data Analysis
- Data Visualization
- Dashboard Design
- Business Insight Generation

# Dashboard Features

## 1. Revenue by Country
Displays total revenue generated by different countries.

### Key Observation
- Australia generated the highest revenue among all countries.

---

## 2. Revenue by Year
Compares yearly revenue performance.

### Key Observation
- Revenue in 2010 was significantly higher than in 2011.

---

## 3. Orders by Year
Shows the number of orders placed each year.

### Key Observation
- 2010 had the majority of total orders.

---

## 4. Top 10 Selling Products
Highlights the best-performing products based on total sales.

### Key Observation
Some products consistently contributed more revenue than others, indicating strong customer demand.

---

## 5. Monthly Orders Analysis
Displays order volume across different months.

### Key Observation
- Orders peaked during the middle months of the year.
- Some months showed a significant drop in customer activity.

---

## 6. Monthly Revenue Analysis
Shows monthly revenue fluctuations.

### Key Observation
- Revenue trends closely followed order trends.
- Certain months generated exceptionally high sales.

---

## 7. Interactive Filters (Slicers)
The dashboard includes filters for:
- Country
- Month
- Year

These slicers allow users to dynamically explore the dataset and perform customized analysis.

# Business Insights

## Revenue Insights
- Australia contributed the largest share of total revenue.
- Revenue concentration in a few countries indicates potential dependence on specific markets.
- Year 2010 outperformed 2011 in both revenue and order count.

## Product Insights
- Top-selling products generated a significant portion of total sales.
- Product demand patterns can help optimize inventory planning.
- High-performing products should be prioritized for promotions and stock management.

## Customer Behavior Insights
- Monthly order fluctuations suggest seasonal purchasing behavior.
- Mid-year months experienced stronger customer activity.
- Lower-performing months may require targeted marketing campaigns.

## Operational Insights
- Interactive filters improve decision-making by enabling focused analysis.
- Dashboard visualization simplifies trend identification and business reporting.
